⚠️ Known limits of this backtest (read once)
• Uses daily closing prices only. The live rule "rising day + buy the dip" happens intraday — here every fill is at the day's close, so entries are approximate.
• Survivorship bias: only today's universe is tested.
• Slippage is a flat % cost per side, not real order-book depth.
• Volume filter uses bridge data when available; falls back to no-filter when volume unavailable.
• Dividends and tracking error are not modelled.

Tax Rules Applied
• STCG 20% — equity ETF gains held ≤ 365 days
• LTCG 12.5% — equity ETF gains held > 365 days (above ₹1.25L exemption per FY)
• Loss offset — losses within FY offset gains before tax calculation
• Carryforward — net loss carried forward to offset future FY gains (up to 8 years)
